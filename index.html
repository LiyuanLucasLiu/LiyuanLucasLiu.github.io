Things I do

... and want to do

I aim to design adaptive algorithms for Resource-Economical machine learning – with a deeper understanding on the underlying mechanisms, algorithms can self-adapt to heterogeneous scenarios, which makes learning more automatic, robust, and thus Resource-Economical. Three major directions of my research:

  • Alleviate the reliance on gold-standard labels. Adapt algorithms to noisy and distant supervision.
  • Reduce trial and error tuning. Allow models self-adapt to different problems/applications.
  • Adapt to different domains with less human endeavors. Capture domain-specific knowledge automatically.
